About Kapë

Where Creativity Meets Ethiopian Excellence

Kape is a Geez word which means "House of Paint" or "Inkhorn." At Kape, we are passionate storytellers dedicated to crafting visually stunning, audibly captivating, and emotionally resonant productions that transform ideas into captivating narratives.

From commercial campaigns to documentary films, our team brings together diverse talents — directors, cinematographers, editors, and designers — all committed to pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling. We believe every project is an opportunity to create something extraordinary.
