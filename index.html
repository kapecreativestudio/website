About Kapë

Where Creativity Meets Excellence

Kapë is a Geez word which means "House of Paint" or "Inkhorn." We are passionate storytellers dedicated to crafting visually stunning productions that transform ideas into narratives.

Based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, we bring world-class production value to every project — from commercial films and documentaries to brand identities and photography.
